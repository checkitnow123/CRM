CheckItNow — Windows Desktop CRM (v1.6.0)
==========================================

WHAT THIS IS
  Offline client & service CRM for tutors, coaches, therapists,
  and small field-service businesses.
  Track renewals, session milestones, reminders, and exports —
  without a cloud subscription.

REQUIREMENTS
  - Windows 10 or 11 (64-bit)
  - Microsoft WebView2 (usually pre-installed)
  - No internet required after install

QUICK START
  1. Extract this entire folder anywhere (e.g. Desktop\CheckItNow)
  2. Double-click CheckItNow.exe
  3. Your data saves in the data\ folder next to the EXE

FOLDERS
  CheckItNow.exe   — launch the app
  config\          — branding & settings (optional customization)
  data\            — your client database & backups (keep safe!)
  _internal\       — runtime files (do not delete)
