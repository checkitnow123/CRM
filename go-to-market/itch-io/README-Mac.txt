CheckItNow — macOS Desktop CRM (v1.6.0)
=========================================

WHAT THIS IS
  Offline client & service CRM for tutors, coaches, therapists,
  and small field-service businesses.
  Track renewals, session milestones, reminders, and exports —
  without a cloud subscription.

REQUIREMENTS
  - macOS 12 (Monterey) or later
  - Apple Silicon (M1/M2/M3) or Intel Mac
  - No internet required after install

QUICK START
  1. Extract this entire folder anywhere (e.g. ~/Applications/CheckItNow)
  2. Double-click CheckItNow.app
  3. Your data saves in the data/ folder next to the app

FIRST LAUNCH (unsigned build)
  If macOS says the app cannot be opened:
  - Right-click CheckItNow.app → Open → Open again
  - Or: System Settings → Privacy & Security → Open Anyway

FOLDERS
  CheckItNow.app   — launch the app
  config/          — branding & settings (optional customization)
  data/            — your client database & backups (keep safe!)
